FlightBase

FlightBase was a personal project that used natural language processing to uncover flight deals in blogs and forums.

These posts often did not include specific dates or flights, so using FlightBase uers were able to search these posts for deals that fit their specific date requirements, and filter results based on price.

See it live at: www.flightbase.co

The Optical Illusion Museum is the first of its kind in Canada, featuring immersive exhibits that encourage audience participation.

The exhibits are natural engagement points on social media, and the idea was to focus on bringing social validation front and center on the website by featuring past visitors' Instagram posts on the home page.
